# Useful Apps/Websites

This is a list of useful apps and websites that can help you in your daily life in Sweden.

## Banks, payments and e-identity

- **Your bank's app** (see [banks](/docs/info/daily-life/banks)) - Most banks have their own app that allows you to manage your account, make payments, and use other services. Make sure to download the app of your bank and set it up.
- **Swish** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.bankgirot.swish)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/swish-payments/id563204724)) - Mobile payment service used by most people in Sweden. You can use it to send and receive money, pay for goods and services, and even pay bills. To use Swish, you need a Swedish bank account and a mobile BankID (see [e-identity](/docs/info/daily-life/e-identity) and [banks](/docs/info/daily-life/banks)).
- **Mobile BankID** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bankid.bus)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/bankid-security-app/id433151512)) - see [e-identity](/docs/info/daily-life/e-identity)
- **Freja eID** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.verisec.mobile.frejaeid)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/freja/id1256552092)) - see [e-identity](/docs/info/daily-life/e-identity)

## Transportation

- **Varmlandstrafik** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.infospread.android.mobitime.r.vtab)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/v%C3%A4rmlandstrafik/id498181254)) - App for public transportation in Värmland. You can use it to plan your trips, buy tickets, and check real-time information about buses and trains.
- **SJ** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.sj.android)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/sj-trains-in-sweden/id442994825)) - App for booking and managing train tickets with SJ, the main train operator in Sweden.
- **VY** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.intele.nsbmob.app)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/vy/id439655098)) - App for booking and managing train tickets with VY, another train operator in Sweden.
- **Ryde** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ryde_android)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/ryde-always-nearby/id1495605028)) - App for renting electric scooters in Karlstad.

## Food and groceries

- **Foodora** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.onlinepizza)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/foodora-food-delivery/id421369701)) - Food delivery service that partners with many local restaurants in Karlstad.
- **Wolt** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wolt.android)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/wolt-delivery-food-and-more/id943905271)) - Another popular food delivery service in Karlstad.
- **ICA** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.ica.handla)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/ica-recept-och-erbjudanden/id458324110)) - App for ICA supermarkets, one of the largest grocery store chains in Sweden. You can use it to find stores, check prices, and create shopping lists.

## Entertainment and media

- **SVT Play** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.svt.android.svtplay)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/svt-play/id343544214)) - App for streaming content from Sveriges Television (SVT), the Swedish public service television company.
- **Sveriges Radio** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.sr.android)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/sveriges-radio-play/id300548244)) - App for streaming content from Sveriges Radio (SR), the Swedish public service radio company.
- **TV4 Play** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.tv4.tv4playtab)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/tv4-play/id413776071)) - App for streaming content from TV4, a popular commercial television channel in Sweden.
- **Telia Play** - App for streaming TV content if you have TV service from Telia.

## Second-hand marketplaces

- **Blocket** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.appcorn.Blocket)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/blocket-k%C3%B6p-s%C3%A4lj-begagnat/id323710525)) - The largest online marketplace for buying and selling second-hand items in Sweden.
- **Tradera** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tradera)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/tradera-buy-sell/id427984084)) - An online auction site where you can buy and sell second-hand items.
- **Facebook Marketplace** - Accessible via the Facebook app or website, it's a popular platform for buying and selling items locally.

## Mail and packages

- **PostNord** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.postnord.private)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/postnord-track-your-parcels/id396871673)) - App for managing your mail and packages with PostNord, the main postal service in Sweden. You can use it to track packages, get notifications, and manage deliveries.
- **Kivra** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.kivra.Kivra)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/kivra/id588705123)) - Digital mailbox service where you can receive and manage your mail electronically. Many Swedish companies and government agencies use Kivra to send important documents.
- **Billo** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=life.billo.app)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/billo-digital-brevl%C3%A5da/id1593173245)) - Another digital mailbox service similar to Kivra, allowing you to receive and manage your mail electronically.
- **Min myndighetspost** ([website](https://minmyndighetspost.se/2.fe04f2018f19befcb967572.html)) - Official digital mailbox service provided by the Swedish government for receiving mail from government agencies.

## Household and recycling

- **Electrolux Vision Mobile** - App for booking laundry room and other facilities at Rud. See [laundry room](/docs/info/accommodation/laundry-room) and [sauna](/docs/info/accommodation/sauna) for more information.
- **Kasta rätt** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.karlstad.kastaratt)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/kasta-r%C3%A4tt/id439654661)) - App by Karlstad Municipality that helps you sort your waste correctly. You can search for items and see how to dispose of them properly.

## Others

- **Mazemap** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=no.trt.ivecto.androidclient.mazemap)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/mazemap/id654064878)) - Indoor navigation app that can help you find your way around the university campus and other large buildings.
- **Yr.no** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=no.nrk.yr)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/yr-no/id490989206)) - Weather forecast service that provides accurate weather information for locations in Norway and Sweden.
- **Mecenat** ([android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.mecenat.app)/[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/it/app/mecenat/id416451110)) - Student discount platform that offers discounts on various products and services for students in Sweden.
